Phase Focus What happens Milestone & notes
Phase 1 ~2 weeks Assessment, Analysis & Design Learning needs assessment, stakeholder interviews, research synthesis, project scoping, and storyboard development. Activities are grouped to enable reverse-engineering from the storyboard output. A decision gate at the end provides an opportunity to course-correct before investment in development. Design milestone A complete blueprint before any content is built. Outputs: assessment report · research report · project plan · storyboard.
Phase 2 ~2 weeks Development, Testing & Delivery Content development, beta testing with real learners, a structured beta feedback session, quality assurance against accessibility and instructional standards, and delivery preparation. Beta feedback is design data, not a formality — it is integrated before deployment. Delivery milestone Content is ready for deployment. Outputs: beta testing report · quality assurance report · delivery preparation plan.
Phase 3 ~2 weeks Evaluation, Impact & Sustainment Post-delivery evaluation, learning path evaluation, impact reporting, and sustainment planning — including update triggers, maintenance cycles, and knowledge management. Evaluation is designed from the start of the project, not retrofitted at the end. Sustainment milestone The program continues to work over time. Outputs: evaluation and impact report · sustainment plan.
